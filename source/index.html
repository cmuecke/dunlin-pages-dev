Dunlin

Video conferencing made fun

What's this?

Imagine video conferencing being simple, just working and fun actually. This is Dunlin. It's a tool to communicate with others via video just inside your browser window. No separate downloads and everything is encrypted by default.

Dunlins focus is ease of use. Share a link with all the members of your conference and you're ready to talk to each other.

No installation

Dunlin only requires you to open a link within your browser. Yup, that's it.

Muting for every single member

For the rare moments when you or your talking frieds need to shut the hell up.

Switch between layout modes

In case our design magic fails to work, Dunlin allows to switch between layout modes to always fullfill your expectations.
